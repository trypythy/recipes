Ingredients:
 - 1 cup water
 - 1 tsp tea
 - 2 tsp sugar
 - 2 tbsp milk
 - little ginger, cardamom, cinnamon(optional)
Directions:
  1. In a medium pan, bring the water to boil.
  2. Add ginger, cardamom, cinnamon & tea and let simmer for about 5 mins.
  3. Add sugar & add a small amount of additional milk.
